### Komorebi
#### Inspired by the late Thich Nhat Hanh.
*A reminder to breathe and stay grounded throughout the day, regardless of the activity or environment around you.*

![Thich Nhat Hanh](photos/IMG_0380.JPG)
*Rest in peace.*

After finishing my degree I wanted to find something that genuinely helped me feel calm and present and listening to his Dharma talks gave me that. This app is a small attempt to carry some of that stillness into daily life â€” a quiet nudge to slow down and breathe, especially as things get busier.
